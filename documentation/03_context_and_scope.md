# Context and Scope
- TurboWin+ supports the Global Observing System (GOS) and climate research through data collected by the Voluntary Observing Ships (VOS).
- It is compatible with various equipment and systems, including GPS and AWS (Automatic Weather Station) interfaces, ensuring interoperability across different observation platforms.
- Via Obs2Server, the observations is immediately sent and on the server of KNMI, the observation will be forwarded as E-mail to Meteo France
